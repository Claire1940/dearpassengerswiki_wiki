Understanding the mechanics of the **dear passengers party game** is the difference between a smooth landing and a fiery descent. The game is divided into two primary phases: the Airport Phase and the Flight Phase. Each requires different skills and levels of cooperation.

| Gameplay Phase | Primary Objectives | Key Challenges |

The physics engine ensures that every movement of the plane affects the players inside, leading to hilarious "pile-ups" in the cabin.
