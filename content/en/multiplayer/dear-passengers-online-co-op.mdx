<Section icon={Target} title="Step-by-Step Flight Survival Guide">

Managing the flight requires executing specific routines in order of urgency. Follow these steps during every shift to maintain control over the aircraft.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Pre-Flight Prep">
    Locate the beverage machines and the repair tool spawners. Ensure both players know where the tools are stored before the passengers board.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Initial Service Run">
    Immediately start brewing coffee and pouring juice. Fill the trays completely so you can serve multiple passengers in a single trip down the aisle.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Mid-Flight Maintenance">
    When the alarm sounds, drop all service items and assist with patching the hull. Structural failures will end the run faster than unhappy passengers.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Emergency Evacuation">
    If the damage is irreversible, abandon service duties. Locate the two crew parachutes and exit the aircraft before it crashes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

During the mid-flight phase, random events like severe turbulence will throw items across the cabin. Secure loose tools in their designated slots to prevent them from becoming unreachable during a crisis.

| Event Type | Immediate Action Required | Tool Needed | Danger Level |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Passenger Alert** | Deliver requested drink | Coffee pot or juice box | **Low** |
| **Wall Crack** | Patch the leaking seam | Repair tape or metal sheet | **High** |
| **Engine Fire** | Extinguish and reboot | Fire extinguisher | **Critical** |
| **Decompression** | Hold onto fixed objects | None | **Critical** |

<Callout type="warning" title="Parachute Scarcity">
There are only enough parachutes for the crew. Do not attempt to distribute them to the passengers; secure them for your partner and yourself to successfully complete the escape phase.
</Callout>
