<Section icon={Swords} title="Co-op Roles and Team Strategy">

A successful flight in the Dear Passengers beta relies on clear division of labor. Since players interact with a highly reactive physics engine, having dedicated roles prevents multiple crew members from abandoning their posts to handle the same emergency.

<CardGrid cols={3}>
  <Card title="The Pilot">
    - **Location**: Cockpit
    - **Duties**: Fly the aircraft, manage weather, steer through air pockets.
    - **Strategy**: Keep the cabin crew informed of upcoming sharp turns or turbulence.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cabin Lead">
    - **Location**: Passenger Cabin
    - **Duties**: Track cabin status, coordinate service, prioritize emergencies.
    - **Strategy**: Direct idle players to clear walkways and secure loose items.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cargo & Containment">
    - **Location**: Cargo Hold / Cabin
    - **Duties**: Monitor high-risk cargo, contain escaped animals, secure luggage.
    - **Strategy**: Perform regular checks on volatile crates during smooth flight segments.
  </Card>
</CardGrid>

For smaller crews, roles must be combined. In a two-player setup, the pilot remains in the cockpit while the second player manages all cabin, cargo, and passenger duties.
