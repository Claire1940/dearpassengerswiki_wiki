Publishers have structured the launch to accommodate different types of players, ranging from casual narrative fans to dedicated collectors. The three main digital tiers provide varying levels of access to post-launch content, cosmetic upgrades, and downloadable media. 

### Standard Edition
The Standard Edition contains the complete base game without any additional digital extras. It is designed for players who want to experience the core narrative campaign and management gameplay at the lowest entry price. 

### Deluxe Edition
For players seeking a deeper dive into the development and atmosphere of the game, the Deluxe Edition adds high-quality digital assets. This version includes the complete digital soundtrack, featuring ambient tracks that set the tone for your journeys, and a digital artbook showcasing early concepts and character designs.

### Supporter Edition
The Supporter Edition is the ultimate digital package, designed for fans who want to show maximum appreciation to the development team. It bundles all Deluxe content alongside exclusive conductor uniform skins, alternative train car decals, and early access to future narrative DLC packs.
