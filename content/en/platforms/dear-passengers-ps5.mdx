<Section icon={Swords} title="Core Mechanics and Combat Systems">

Mastering **dear passengers ps5** requires understanding that you are rarely just a pilot. Every player must be ready to swap roles at a moment's notice. The combat system is surprisingly robust, featuring firearms like the AK-47 to repel attackers or deal with "problematic" cargo. 

The physics of the plane are affected by the cargo's weight and position. If everyone moves to the back of the plane to deal with an escaped alligator, the nose will pitch up, potentially stalling the aircraft. Balancing your team's physical presence is just as important as balancing the manifest.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Opening the cargo door while in mid-flight will cause a massive decompression event. Ensure all teammates are tethered or holding onto a fixed object before hitting the release switch, or you will lose more than just your cargo.
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Survival Strategies for High-Altitude Chaos">

Survival in **dear passengers ps5** is about mitigating disasters. You won't be able to stop every fire or catch every alligator, but you can choose which ones to prioritize. The "Nuke" cargo, for instance, requires constant cooling or careful handling to prevent an immediate "Game Over."

When the plane inevitably begins to blow up, your goal shifts from delivery to survival. Parachutes are limited, and sometimes the only way to survive is to stay inside the cabin "shell" as it falls. Coordination via game chat is the only way to decide who gets the last parachute and who tries to ride the wreckage down.

<Callout type="success" title="Survival Tip">
If the plane is losing altitude rapidly, jettisoning heavy cargo like the "Kilo" crates or the Alligators can buy you precious minutes to restart the engines.
</Callout>

| Hazard | Immediate Action | Long-term Solution |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Engine Fire** | Use extinguisher | Divert power to secondary thrusters |
| **Escaped Alligator** | Use AK-47 or bait | Secure in reinforced crates |
| **Hull Breach** | Patch with duct tape | Descend to lower altitude |
| **Nuclear Timer** | Manual override | Eject cargo immediately |
